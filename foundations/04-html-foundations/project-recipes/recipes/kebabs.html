Grilled Meat Kebabs

Tender chunks of marinated meat and fresh vegetables skewered and grilled to perfection. These delicious kebabs are ideal for summer barbecues and make a fun, interactive meal for everyone.

Ingrediants

  • 900g beef sirloin, cubed
  • 2 bell peppers, chunked
  • 1 large red onion, chunked
  • 60ml olive oil and lemon juice marinade
  • Salt, pepper, and garlic powder to taste

Steps

  1. Combine olive oil and lemon juice in a bowl with garlic powder, salt, and pepper
  2. Add beef cubes to marinade and refrigerate for 2 hours
  3. Preheat barbecue to medium-high heat
  4. Thread marinated beef, peppers, and onions onto skewers alternating ingredients
  5. Grill kebabs for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally until meat is cooked through
  6. Let rest for 2 minutes before serving
  7. Serve hot with your favorite sides
